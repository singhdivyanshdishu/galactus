## üí° Tips

- Hold `Super+Shift+/` to see the hotkey overlay
- Most shortcuts work with both arrow keys and vim-style keys (hjkl)
- Window management is column-based - think in terms of columns, not individual windows
- Use `Super+R` to cycle through preset column widths (25%, 33%, 50%, 67%, 75%)
